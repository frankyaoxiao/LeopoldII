Goals For the Future

Expand the Belgian Empire

As a fierce believer in the greatness of Belgium, one of Leopold's primary goals will be to expand the empire, particularly in the African region. Leopold believes that doing so will enrich both our nation and theirs, as we bring modern technologies and practices over to them in the process of our conquest.

Improve the Belgium infrastructure

On the domestic front, one of Leopold's primary goals will be to improve the infrastructure here in Belgium, a project that is already in the undertaking, as can be shown in the various building already erected.

Reform the Military and protect our country

As a veteran of the army, Leopold II knows the ins and outs of the Belgian army. Leopold believes that in order to maintain peace and protect our country, we must push for a stronger military. This is why one of the top items on his agenda will be to reform the military, and to modernize every weapon in its arsenal.
