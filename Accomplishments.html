Accomplishments

A Defender of the State

At the young age of 9, Leopold received the title of Duke of Brabant, and he served in the army until he ascended to the throne. By that time, he had reached the rank of Lieutenant-General. His experiences in the military would come to shape his views on military and the defense of state as he became king.

A Man of the People

On the home front, Leopold II has done much for the Belgium public. He enacted policies benefitting the working class, such as the right to unionize, as well as universal male suffrage. As a ruler, Leopold has increased the say of the everyday Belgian in how the country was run.

A True Belgian

As a fierce believer in Belgian greatness, Leopold has taken steps to acquire land for our country in Africa. Overseas colonies, such as those in Africa, will be the key to the continued power and greatness of the Belgian Empire.
