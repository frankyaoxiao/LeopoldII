Accomplishments

As the King of the Belgians, Leopold has accomplished many things. Here are the highlights

Army Service

As a young man, Leopold was an officer in the Belgian army, serving tirelessly to further the glory of Belgium.

Universal male suffrage

As King, Leopold revised the constitution of Belgium to include voting rights to all men, no matter what walks of life they come from.

Champion of the Workers

As a man of the people, Leopold passed laws that allowed workers the unionize, as well as abolishing the scourge known as child labor.

Great Conquerer

As a firm believer of colonies, Leopold expanded Belgium's reach into Africa.

Defender of Belgium

As the King of Belgium, Leopold built up many great defensive fortresses in order to preserve our nation.

Builder King

As King, Leopold commissioned many urban buildings and public works, including the Hippodrome Wellington Racetrack and the Royal Museum for Central Africa.
